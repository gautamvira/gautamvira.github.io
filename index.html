ABOUT ME

Currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science at the University of Ottawa with a focus on predictive maintenance for public buses by leveraging IoT devices, I have been actively involved in cutting-edge research at the Smart IoT Lab. My experience includes decoding and analyzing sensor data, implementing machine learning models such as Generative Adversarial Networks and LSTM networks, and utilizing representation learning techniques for predictive maintenance in a real-world setting.
In addition to my research experience, I have served as a Teaching Assistant at the University of Ottawa, where I honed my communication and leadership skills by creating and delivering labs and tutorials for undergraduate computer science and engineering courses. This role has equipped me with the ability to convey complex technical concepts in a clear and concise manner, ensuring the success of students with diverse learning styles.
