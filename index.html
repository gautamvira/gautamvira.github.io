ABOUT ME

As a dedicated R&D Engineer and computer science professional, I am passionate about using AI and data-driven solutions to solve complex problems and drive innovation. With a strong academic foundation, including a Master’s in Computer Science (Thesis) from the University of Ottawa, I have honed my skills in developing predictive models, conducting advanced data analysis, and deploying machine learning solutions that deliver real-world impact.
I’ve had the privilege of working on cutting-edge projects such as predictive maintenance for IoT-enabled public buses, Tor application detection using AI-based classifiers, and enhancing neural network models with advanced optimization techniques. My hands-on experience in research and development is complemented by my role as a Teaching Assistant, where I have taught and mentored hundreds of students in core computer science, electrical engineering, and software engineering concepts.
Driven by curiosity and a commitment to excellence, I am always eager to explore new opportunities in AI, machine learning, and data science. I am particularly interested in roles that combine innovative technologies with impactful problem-solving to make a tangible difference in industries.
